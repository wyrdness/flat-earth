## Overview

The Flat Earth belief claims that Earth is a flat plane rather than the scientifically proven oblate spheroid (slightly flattened sphere). Despite overwhelming evidence spanning over 2,000 years proving Earth's spherical shape, the belief experienced a modern resurgence through internet communities and YouTube algorithms.

**Common Claims**:
- Earth is flat disc or plane, not sphere
- NASA and governments hide the truth
- Space exploration is faked
- Antarctica forms ice wall barrier around edge

**Reality**: Earth is demonstrably spherical. Evidence includes:
- Ships disappearing hull-first over horizon
- Star positions changing with latitude
- Time zones and day/night cycles
- Circumnavigation by sea and air
- Satellite technology (GPS, communications)
- Photographs from space (multiple independent agencies)
- Physics and gravity
- Ancient measurements (Eratosthenes, 240 BCE)

The modern resurgence represents science denial and often serves as a gateway to other conspiracy theories.

## Flat Earth Models

### Common Models

| Model | Description | Antarctica | Prevalence |
|-------|-------------|-----------|-----------|
| **Disc with dome** | Flat disc with dome-shaped sky; sun/moon/stars rotate inside | Ice wall forms edge | Most common |
| **Infinite plane** | Earth extends infinitely; known world is small section | Ice wall marks boundary; more land beyond | Less common |
| **Disc with edge** | Finite disc with reachable edge | Ice wall or barrier prevents reaching edge | Rare |

### Common Elements

Flat Earth models typically include:

- ✗ Sun and moon are small and local (not millions of miles away)
- ✗ Stars are lights in dome/firmament, not distant suns
- ✗ Gravity doesn't exist (replaced by "density" or "buoyancy")
- ✗ Antarctica is guarded ice wall barrier
- ✗ Space exploration is faked (NASA, other agencies)
- ✗ Globe Earth is conspiracy by governments/scientists/elites

## Alleged "Evidence" (Debunked)

### Visual Claims

| Claim | Description | Reality |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Horizon appears flat** | Ocean/flat land horizon looks flat | Earth too large for curvature to be obvious at ground level; curve visible from high altitude |
| **Water doesn't curve** | Water finds level, can't stick to spinning ball | Gravity pulls water toward Earth's center, creating spherical surface |
| **No curve in photography** | Distant objects visible that should be hidden | Atmospheric refraction bends light, allowing visibility beyond geometric horizon |
| **No airplane adjustment** | Planes don't adjust nose down for curve | Planes fly at constant altitude above curved surface; gravity provides constant pull |
| **Stars don't change enough** | Star positions should change more if orbiting sun | Stellar parallax measurable but small due to vast distances; measured since 1838 |

### Conspiracy Claims

| Claim | Alleged Evidence | Reality |
|-------|------------------|---------|
| **NASA fakes space** | Anomalies in photos, CGI claims | Photos explained by camera physics; independent space agencies worldwide |

| **Global conspiracy** | Universal acceptance of globe Earth | Scientific consensus based on evidence; impossible worldwide conspiracy |
| **Control/deception** | Globe Earth for control | No motive or mechanism; Earth's shape doesn't enable control |

## Scientific Evidence (Earth is Spherical)

### Ancient Knowledge

**Educated people have known Earth is spherical for over 2,000 years:**

| Date | Person | Contribution |
|------|--------|--------------|
| **~500 BCE** | Pythagoras | Proposed spherical Earth based on observations |
| **~350 BCE** | Aristotle | Provided evidence: ships disappear hull-first, changing stars, circular shadow on moon |
| **~240 BCE** | Eratosthenes | **Calculated Earth's circumference** with remarkable accuracy using shadow angles |

**Note**: The idea that medieval people believed in flat Earth is a **modern myth**. Medieval scholars knew Earth was spherical.

### Overwhelming Modern Evidence

#### 1. Ships Disappear Hull-First

**Observation**: Ships disappear over horizon bottom-first  
**What it proves**: Earth's surface curves  
**Debunks flat Earth**: On flat plane, entire ship would remain visible shrinking into distance  
**How to verify**: Watch ships near ocean horizon with binoculars  

#### 2. Star Positions Change with Latitude

**Observation**: Different stars visible from different latitudes; southern/northern hemispheres see different stars  
**What it proves**: Observer's position on spherical surface  
**Debunks flat Earth**: Only possible on sphere; flat plane would show same stars from all positions  
**How to verify**: Travel north-south and observe stars  

#### 3. Time Zones

**Observation**: Sunset/sunrise occurs at different times across globe  
**What it proves**: Earth rotates, creating day/night cycle  
**Debunks flat Earth**: Flat Earth models can't explain simultaneous day/night  
**How to verify**: Call someone in different time zone during your daytime - confirm it's nighttime there  

#### 4. Circumnavigation

**Observation**: Traveling continuously east or west returns to starting point  
**What it proves**: Earth is a closed spherical surface  
**Debunks flat Earth**: Impossible on flat plane with edge  
**How to verify**: Book circumnavigation flight; observe continuous travel  

#### 5. Satellite Technology

**Observation**: GPS, communications, weather satellites function based on spherical Earth orbits  
**What it proves**: Orbital mechanics require spherical Earth  
**Debunks flat Earth**: System only functions with spherical Earth  
**How to verify**: Use GPS (billions use it daily)  

#### 6. Photographs from Space

**Observation**: Thousands of photos from multiple space agencies, private companies  
**What it proves**: Direct visual confirmation  
**Debunks flat Earth**: Independent agencies from competing nations all show spherical Earth  
**Sources**: NASA, ESA, Roscosmos, CNSA, JAXA, SpaceX, Blue Origin  

#### 7. Physics and Gravity

**Observation**: Gravity pulls objects toward center of mass, creating sphere  
**What it proves**: All large celestial bodies spherical due to gravity  
**Debunks flat Earth**: Flat Earth violates fundamental physics  
**How to verify**: All planets and moons observed are spherical  

#### 8. Lunar Eclipse

**Observation**: Earth's shadow on moon is always circular  
**What it proves**: Only sphere casts circular shadow from all angles  
**Debunks flat Earth**: Flat disc would cast elliptical shadow at angles  
**How to verify**: Observe any lunar eclipse  

#### 9. Coriolis Effect

**Observation**: Rotation causes deflection of moving objects; affects weather, ballistics  
**What it proves**: Earth rotates on axis  
**Debunks flat Earth**: Effect only occurs on rotating sphere  
**How to verify**: Hurricanes rotate differently in hemispheres

## History

### Ancient to Medieval

**Myth**: "People used to believe in flat Earth"  
**Reality**: Educated people have known Earth is spherical for over 2,000 years. Medieval scholars knew Earth was spherical. The "flat Earth medieval belief" is itself a modern myth.

### Modern Resurgence

| Period | Event | Significance |
|--------|-------|--------------|
| **1956** | Samuel Shenton founds Flat Earth Society (UK) | Modern organized movement begins (very small) |
| **1970s-90s** | Charles K. Johnson leads Flat Earth Society (USA) | American advocacy (remained fringe) |
| **2000s** | Internet communities form | Online forums allow believers to connect |
| **2014-2016** | YouTube algorithm amplification | **Explosive growth** via recommended videos |
| **2017-present** | Conferences, celebrity endorsements | Peak visibility; mainstream media coverage |

### YouTube Radicalization

**Critical Factor**: YouTube's recommendation algorithm heavily promoted flat Earth videos in mid-2010s

**Mechanism**:
1. User watches conspiracy-related content
2. Algorithm recommends increasingly extreme videos
3. Users exposed to flat Earth claims
4. Algorithmic echo chamber reinforces belief

**Impact**: Rapid growth in flat Earth belief; many believers cite YouTube as introduction

**Response**: YouTube later adjusted algorithm to reduce conspiracy theory promotion

## Debunking

### Logical Impossibility

Flat Earth models **cannot explain**:

❌ Time zones (why is it day in America when night in China?)  
❌ Seasons (why do hemispheres have opposite seasons?)  
❌ Star positions (why do different latitudes see different stars?)  
❌ Satellite technology (GPS requires spherical orbit)  
❌ Circumnavigation (how do you travel continuously east and return?)  
❌ Physics (why would Earth be only flat object when all other celestial bodies are spherical?)  

**Every flat Earth "explanation" requires ad-hoc special pleading and contradicts other flat Earth claims.**

### The Bottom Line

**Earth is an oblate spheroid** (slightly flattened sphere).

This is scientific **fact** proven by overwhelming evidence from multiple independent fields:
- Astronomy
- Physics  
- Geology
- Navigation
- Space exploration
- Ancient measurements

**Evidence for flat Earth**: None  
**Evidence for spherical Earth**: Overwhelming and independently verifiable
